Kanad Merchant Guide

This guide helps merchants configure the Kanad theme after installation.

Getting Started

  1. Upload your logo and favicon in Theme settings.
  2. Set your brand colors and typography in Theme settings.
  3. Choose your main menu and footer menu.
  4. Populate your homepage sections with collections, products, and imagery.

Key Features

  • Predictive search for products, collections, pages, and articles
  • Collection and search filtering
  • Localization selectors for country and language
  • Gift card recipient support
  • Selling plan support on product and order surfaces

Recommended Setup

  • Create a main menu with nested collection links.
  • Configure at least one featured collection on the homepage.
  • Use products with multiple variants, discounts, and subscriptions for the best presentation.
  • Publish all target languages and markets before enabling localization selectors.
