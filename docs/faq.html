FAQ

Does Kanad support Shopify markets?

Yes. The theme includes country and language selectors when multiple markets or languages are enabled.

Does Kanad support subscriptions?

Yes. Selling plans are available on supported subscription products and are shown in cart and order views.

Does Kanad include predictive search?

Yes. Predictive search is enabled in the header and search page interface.
